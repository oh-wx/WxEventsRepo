...Storm System Will Bring Wintry Weather to the Region...

.A low pressure system will move northeast through the central
Great Lakes Saturday. The combination of the cold air in place
and plenty of moisture with the storm system will result in snow
initially. The snow will become mixed with or change over to
freezing rain by Saturday morning for a period of time before
becoming all rain. The low pressure system will move northeast of
the area taking the precipitation with it Saturday night.
